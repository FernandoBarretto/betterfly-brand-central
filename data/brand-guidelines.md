## Typography

### Font Families
- **Display / Hero headlines:** Alumni Sans (ExtraBold, weight 800)
- **Everything else (headings, body, labels, buttons):** Inter (Regular 400 / Medium 500 / SemiBold 600 / Bold 700)

### Font Weights
| Weight | Value | Usage |
|---|---|---|
| Regular | 400 | Body text, default labels, paragraphs |
| Medium | 500 | Emphasized labels, medium-weight buttons |
| SemiBold | 600 | Headings, prominent labels |

## Brand Tone

- **We sound:** Empowering, warm, clear
- **We never sound:** Corporate, cold, jargon-heavy
- **Headline style:** Benefit-first, action-oriented, no filler words
- **Voice principle:** Lead with the employee or employer benefit — not the product feature

---

## Layout Principles

- Use clear visual hierarchy: headline → supporting stat or proof point → CTA
- White space is intentional — avoid crowded layouts
- Always include Betterfly brand lockup when presenting externally
- Dark surface sections use green950 (#042914) or forest950 (#0F1C14) as background with white text
- Footer on all generated assets: "Generated by Betterfly Brand Central · Internal use only"

---

## Output Format Rules (Asset Generator)

- **One-Pager:** Single column, max 3 sections, one strong visual or stat
- **Two-Pager:** Left side visual/headline, right side body + CTA
- **Short Deck:** 5–7 slides max, each slide carries one idea
- **LinkedIn Post:** 3–4 short paragraphs, end with a question or CTA
